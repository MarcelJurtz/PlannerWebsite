Centralized Eventmanagement

Tired of having to reach out to your members via email, messenger and other messaging channels? This is now a thing of the past! Our software allows you to use your own platform to manage events and to evaluate confirmations and cancellations. And all this in your very own style!

Our Software offers

  • Central, web-based management of your events
  • Direct exchange with your club members
  • Team-based event management, each member only sees the relevant entries
  • User roles to differentiate between users and administrators
  • Exchange of news on the landing page for fast, general communication

Functionality

Our solution offers you the possibility to manage your events according to your wishes. This includes:

  • Creating and managing teams and assigning users
  • Management of events and individual assignment of events for teams
  • Optional specification of roles for events (e.g. player, umpire, sales etc.)
  • Clear evaluation of approvals and rejections for simple task distribution

The software can be accessed from anywhere via the Internet, but only registered members can use it. Graphically you have the option of a customization (color style & logo) according to your wishes.
